Q1. How can I start monitoring my periods?

A1. You can add the period information by simply tapping the callout or balloon on the homescreen to enter the period start and end date. You can also tap on the period log button on the homepage to show you the complete screen from where you can easily manage the previous period logs and also add the new period records.

Q2. How can I check the prediction for my period, fertile and ovulation days ?

A2. After entering your period log, application will start predicting and displaying the possible fertile and ovulation days on the top section of homescreen itself on the basis of default dates set by you in settings. You can also check the detailed view of the ovulation and fertile from the Period Log section. Simply click on the prediction button on the period log screen to get the predictions of next 6 months for Period, Fertile and Ovulation.

Q3. How can I add or change the notes, symptoms and height, weight information ?

A3. Simply click the Add Notes button on the homescreen and start entering the various details for the day. You can also change the date simply by clicking the left or right arrow button available on the top in the notes screen. You can also add notes and other details by clicking any of the date in the calendar to enter the details for the selected date.

Q4. How can I see the complete month view ?

A4. Click Calendar icon on the homescreen to display the calendar view of the application. By default the current month calendar would be displayed with the current date selected. Calendar Screen will display the complete month view with dates highlighted with period, fertile and ovulation. Clicking on any date will display the notes and other information below the calendar. You can also click the notes below the calendar to add or edit the notes.

Q5. How does application predict my menstrual cycle ?

A5. The application uses the famous Standard Days Method to predict the next cycle.

Q6. How to deactivate menstrual cycle monitoring mode when I am pregnant.

A6. You can activate the pregnancy mode by selecting the pregnancy option from the settings screen. Once the pregnancy mode is activated application will display you the estimated due date on the basis of the last Menstrual period provided. Once the pregnancy mode is on, app will not allow to you to enter further period logs but you can still add or update the past log records. You can also change the estimated date from the pregnancy screen.

Q7. How can I setup the application password ?

A7. You can select the password option under the settings to the set the password. Everytime the application starts will ask you to enter the pin/password to continue.

Q8. What if I do not find the answer here ?

A8. In case you do not find any answer here, please feel free to mail us your question to our customer support service at myperiodtracker@lptpl.com. The team will revert back to you as soon as possible.

Q9. How can i change the default values for cycle length, period length and ovulation day ?

A9. You can change the default values under the settings option of the app. App will allow you to set the default value as per your custom needs.

Q10. How can I change the language in the application ?

A10. You can select the language option from the settings menu. As of now app is supporting 10 different languages. Support of other languages are in progress and will be updated soon.
